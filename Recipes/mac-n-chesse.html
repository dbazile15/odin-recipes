Creamy Mac and Cheese

So full disclosure I am not chef, but I watch a lot of cooking content on YouTube. This is one that I made. I am not the biggest fan of baked mac and cheese and I prefer mine creamy. That is the recipe you are seeing below

Ingredients

  • Kosher Salt
  • Small Elbow Macoroni
  • Unsalted Butter
  • All purpose flour
  • CHicken Boulion Powder
  • Paprika
  • Onion Powder
  • Whole milk
  • Heavy Cream
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • American Cheese
  • Gouda Cheese

Steps

  1. Get water to a boil
  2. Add a generous amount of Kosher Salt
  3. Add 8oz of Small Elbow Macoroni
  4. In a separate pot on medium heat add 2 table spoons of unsalted butter
  5. Once melted add two tablespoons of your flour Stir often
  6. Add 2 teaspoons of chicken boulion powder, half a teaspoon of paprika and half a teaspoon of onion powder
  7. Add 1 and a quarter cup of whole milk
  8. Add half cup of heavy cream
  9. Shred 1 cup of Cheddar Cheese and 1 cup of American Cheese
  10. Shred half a cup of Gouda Cheese
  11. Combine all Cheese in a cup
  12. Cut off the heat. Add half of your cheese into your separate pot(the second one)
  13. Once melted add the rest of your cheese until melted
  14. Remove a cup of your pasta water and set to the side
  15. Drain your pasta
  16. Add pasta to Cheese mix
  17. If the cheese is too think you can smoothen it with the pasta water
