So full disclosure I am not chef, but I watch a lot of cooking content on YouTube. So this recipe is one that I found on youtube and once I have access to a kitchen, will be one of the first recipes that I try out.

Marinade Ingredients

  • Buttermilk
  • Salt

Dredge Dry Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • Cornstarch
  • Baking powder
  • Paprika
  • Dry Mustard
  • Chicken Boullion Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Onion Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Thyme
  • Smoked Paprika
  • Peanut Oil but for frying

Steps

  1. Grab your chicken and place it in a bowl
  2. Submerge it in buttermilk and salt mixture for 6 hours
  3. Add all of the ingridients from Dredge list to a shallow but large bowl
  4. Take a few spoonfuls of your marinade and add it to the dry ingridents and mix it by hand
  5. Add Peanut oil to a heavy bottom pot
  6. Remove a piece of chicken from the marinade and shake off excess buttermilk
  7. Place on Dredge Dry ingridents and fully coat making sure to squeeze into the flour mix
  8. Shake off excess flour
  9. Place on a wire rack until ready to fry
  10. When oil gets to 350 degrees you want to add the coated chicken to the oil in batches of 3
  11. Fry until golen brown and set aside on a towel lined baking sheet
  12. As soon as they come out add salt to them
  13. Add them to a wire lined baking sheet and finish in the oven
