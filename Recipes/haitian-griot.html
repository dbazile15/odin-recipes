Haitan Griot

So full disclosure I am not chef, but I watch a lot of cooking content on YouTube. So this recipe is one that I found on youtube and once I have access to a kitchen, I can't wait to try this out.

Ingredients

  • Pork Shoulder
  • Lime
  • Chicken Boulion Cube
  • Pack of Sazon
  • 1 Tomato
  • Haitian Spice Epiz
  • Jalapenoz poked with Cloves
  • Parlsely tied with Thyme
  • Pieces of Habenero Peper
  • Bitter or Sour Orange Juice
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Peper

Steps

  1. Clean Pork Shoulder using Hot Water and Lime
  2. Set Stove to medium High with Pork shoulder in the pot
  3. Add your Epiz
  4. Mix thoughougly
  5. Add Chicken Boulion Cube and Sazon Packet
  6. Mix until combined
  7. Add 3 capfuls of bitter orange juice
  8. Cut tomato in to five pieces and add to pot
  9. Add Habenero, Jalapenoz, thyme and parsley to pot
  10. Add Salt to taste
  11. Add 1 cup of water to the pot and let it cook for 45 minutes
  12. Fry in a pan of Oil until it is a golen brown color
